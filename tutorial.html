Tutorial: Mastering Acrylic Nails

Welcome to our beginner's guide to achieving stunning acrylic nails. Our step-by-step tutorial will help you master the art of acrylic nail application, so get ready to transform your nails into a stunning look!

A step by step guide:

1. Selecting the proper size: Begin by identifying the right size of acrylic nails for each of your natural nails. This ensures a proper and comfortable fit.

2. Preparing your natural nail: Get your nails ready by cleaning, and filing them. When cleaning the cuticles, using a cuticle remover while filing is the way to go. For more information on the cuticle remover, check out our list of recommended supplies. Once you're ready, apply a thin layer of either a prep booster or a base coat on your natural nail.

3. Applying nail glue to natural nail: Put a thin layer of nail glue on your prepared natural nails. This will make sure the acrylic nails stick securely.

4. Drying under LED lamp: Use an LED lamp to dry the glue on your natural nails, for 10 - 15 seconds. This step is crucial for creating a strong bond between your natural nails and the acrylics.

5. Applying nailglue on acrylic nails: Apply nail glue to the back of the acrylic nails, ensuring even coverage for a secure attachment.

6. Attach the acrylic nail: Gently press the acrylic nail onto the glued natural nail, holding it in place for a moment. After sensing the acrylic nail is in good positioning, use the mini LED lamp to dry it for 15 seconds, ensuring a secure connection. Note: For use of proper images, the LED lamp in the picture is not illuminated, but make sure to turn it on during this step!

7. Trimming and shaping the acrylic nail: Use nail clippers and a file to trim and shape the acrylic nail to your preferred length and style.

8. Painting the acrylic nail: Now, it's time to apply nail polish. Choose a gel polish of your liking and apply multiple layers until you're satisfied with the color contrast. Remember to use the LED lamp to dry each layer for one minute before proceeding to the next.

9. Applying top coat: Apply a layer of top coat over the acrylic nail for protection and durability. This will also make your nails look glossy.

10. Drying under LED lamp(1 minute): Cure the top coat by drying your nails under an LED lamp for one minute. This ensures a fully set and glossy finish.

11. Repeating the process: Repeat the entire process for the remaining nails, and you're done!

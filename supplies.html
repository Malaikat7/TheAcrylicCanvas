Nail Care Essentials: Top Picks for Stunning Nails

Welcome to the collection of our handpicked recommendations, to help you enhance the health and beauty of your nails. Explore our list of supplies and take a step further towards top-quality results!

Our recommendations:

AIMEILI Base Coat, Top Coat and Matte Top Coat set: For achieving the best final touches, we suggest this AIMEILI set including three different coats. The base coat serves as a protective layer for your natural nails, while the top coats are used for the final touch. We also encourage you to experiment with the matte top coat to give your nails a different appearance! To acquire this product, a simple internet search is all it takes.

Cleaning Brush: Filing your nails can get pretty messy, and having dust on your nails is something we would all like to avoid. In that case, a simple brush can be of great help after you've filed your nails! You don't necessarily need to invest in a specialized brush; make-up brushes work just as well. Make sure that the brush is sufficiently large and has a slightly fluffy texture for effective cleanup.

Acrylic Nail Clipper: When it's time to shape your acrylic nails, using a regular nail clipper can result in a messy outcome. To achieve a clean and even cut, it's recommended to go for a dedicated acrylic nail clipper. This ensures precision and a smooth finish with just one cut! Typically, you can find this tool included in acrylic nail sets sold on various online stores. However, it's also available for individual purchase.

GELLEN Cuticle Oil: Once you've completed the nail styling, you might notice the skin around your nails feeling dry or rough. To address this, applying a cuticle oil into your routine is an excellent way to maintain healthy and hydrated skin. The featured brand in the image is GELLEN, which is highly recommended, but any cuticle oil, regardless of the brand, will work. If you prefer not to make a purchase, using Vaseline is also a great alternative to keep your skin moisturized.

Cuticle Remover: This is a product we recommend to everyone who's interested in doing their own acrylic nails; an absolute game changer! Before putting on your acrylic nails, prepping your natural nails is a must. With this cuticle remover, you'll be able to have the cleanest nails. Not only does this contribute to the overall health of your nails, but it also ensures a proper fit of the acrylic nails onto your natural ones. You can easily locate and order this product online with a swift internet search.

Acrylic Nail Stand: You might have come across this technique in various tutorial videos. Placing your acrylic nails on nail stands allows you to concentrate fully on perfecting your designs. Instead of polishing the acrylic nails after gluing them onto your natural nails, you can now create your designs without any concerns. Nail stands are accessible on various online stores.

Prep Booster: This Pink Gellac prep booster is a great way to make sure your nails will last long! Applying a thin layer of this polish on your natural nails eliminates any excess oil, leaving your nails clean and primed for the application of acrylic nails. You can easily purchase this product through the Pink Gellac app or at their physical stores.

Nail Tips Display: This display is incredibly convenient for experimenting with colors. After applying the polish in your usual manner, you can simply note down the color number. This way, you'll be able to easily identify and recreate the desired color whenever you wish. You can find this product on the Pink Gellac app and at their physical stores.
